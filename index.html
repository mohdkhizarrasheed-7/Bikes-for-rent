At Your Service

Listing

Choose weather you want to rent out an old vehicle or invest in a new one then, fill out a simple form to provide your vehicle details with us. Rentelo's team will coordinate with you to inform you about the next steps.

Approval

After you list your vehicle on Rentelo's platform, our team will review and approve your listing. Rentelo's team will coordinate with you to inform you about the next steps based on your preference.

Track

Rentelo provides you with an admin panel to track all your bookings, monitor your vehicle's location, and see how much you're earning every day, ensuring transparency and control.

Earn

And you are all set! Rentelo will start promoting your vehicle and take care of all the bookings. Rentelo's team will coordinate with you to inform you about the next steps based on your preference.
